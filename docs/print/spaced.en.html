SPACED REPETITION is one of the best evidence based learning techniques. The principle behind it is to revise learned material in progressively increasing intervals to better commit it to the long-term memory. The length of your course entirely depends on you, however we recommend minimum of 4 months long commitment. It is preferable to calculate and schedule the revision dates beforehand in your calendar.
