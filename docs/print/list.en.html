The Georgian alphabet shown in the chart is a bicameral style proposed by the renowned linguist Akaki Shanidze in the 20th century. The writing standard combines historic Asomtavruli capital letters with modern-day unicase Mkhedruli as its minuscule. This infographic was created by www.anbani.ge.
